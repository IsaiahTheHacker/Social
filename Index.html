The horror of Sweatshops

By Isaiah Edgecombe

Sweatshops are immoral, and should be illegal.

What is a sweatshop?

A sweat shop is a factory line that is extreamly crowded, has very poor or even illigal working conditions, and provides very little pay.

Why is this a problem?

Sweatshops are highly immoral due to the fact that the workers are misstreated, put in dangourus working conditions, and not payed enough money to live.
